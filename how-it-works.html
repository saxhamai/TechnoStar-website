Our 5-Step Repair Journey

A systematic approach to solve hardware and software issues.

01

Problem Identification

You bring the device or we pick it up. We listen to the specific issues (e.g., overheating, dead screen, slow speed) and perform a physical check.

02

Diagnosis & Estimation

We open the device to find the root cause (chip-level). We inform you about the exact cost. We start work ONLY after your approval.

03

Expert Repair

Technicians fix the issue using genuine parts. This includes soldering, component replacement, thermal paste application, and deep cleaning.

04

QC Testing

We run stress tests (benchmark) to ensure the system is stable, temperature is normal, and the issue is permanently resolved.

05

Delivery & Warranty

You verify the device performance yourself. Once satisfied, you make the payment. We provide a service warranty for peace of mind.
